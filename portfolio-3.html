Golden Signals Observability

Implemented comprehensive observability strategy based on Google's Golden Signals (latency, traffic, errors, saturation) for high-criticality banking applications. Configured Dynatrace dashboards and automated alerting for real-time system health monitoring.

Defined and tracked Service Level Indicators (SLIs) and Service Level Objectives (SLOs) to ensure 99.9% uptime. Integrated with PagerDuty for automated incident response and reduced mean time to resolution (MTTR) by 40%.
